The Story

A vanishing craft,
worn into the future.

Silver filigree — the ancient art of weaving precious metal into gossamer lace — once adorned the courts of Mughal emperors and European royalty. Today, fewer than a handful of workshops worldwide still practice this technique at this level of complexity.

When you wear this butterfly, you are not just wearing jewellery. You are carrying forward a tradition that human hands might lose to history.
